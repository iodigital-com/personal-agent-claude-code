## Purpose by Cadence

**Daily** — log what happened, what you thought, what you captured. Low effort, high frequency.

**Weekly** — review the week: what moved, what's stuck, what's next. The most useful unit for pattern recognition.

**Monthly** — zoom out: what shifted in projects, areas, values, goals. The right level for course correction.
