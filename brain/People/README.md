## Meeting Notes

Meeting notes live inline in the person's note (for 1:1s) or as standalone notes for group meetings:
- `People/YYYY-MM-DD Meeting With Person.md`
- Or use `_Templates/Meeting.md` for multi-person meetings

## Privacy Note

This vault may be version-controlled. Be thoughtful about what you record about others — keep notes about your own reactions and actions rather than private details about other people.
